The Photos tool enables us to easily capture, store, and share project photos in a secure online archive. Upload from your PC or use your smartphone or tablet to take a progress photo, add markups, and instantly share with team members. This is helpful for creating reports, checking progress, and showcasing our APAC project works.

What the Photos tool can do
📷

Capture from any device

Upload photos from your smartphone, tablet, or computer - with unlimited storage organised into albums.

📍

Automatic GPS location

The Map view pinpoints exactly where each photo was taken using GPS data automatically extracted from the file.

🔓

Controlled access

Set photos to Private (visible to internal Tesla users only) or share selected images with external stakeholders.

📊

Exportable reports

Generate photo reports with 1, 2, or 4 photos per page - ideal for stakeholder updates and progress reporting.

See it in action - real project screenshots

The screenshots below are taken directly from Procore on our projects. Follow along to see exactly how the Photos tool works from start to finish.

1
Find Photos in the project tools menu
Navigate to your project in Procore, open the tools menu, and select Photos under Project Management.

The Photos tool sits under Project Management - look for it starred in your favourites once you’ve used it a few times.

2
Explore the Photos interface
The Photos tool has four views: Map, Timeline, Albums, and Recycle Bin. On the right, use Upload and Export. Subscribe to follow a project’s photo stream.

The toolbar gives you full control - tabs on the left, Upload and Export on the right.

3
Upload your photos
Click Upload and drag & drop your photos. The system automatically extracts GPS location data. Always set to Private and choose an album.

The upload dialog: choose your album, confirm Private is ticked, then click Upload photos.

4
Browse your Timeline
The Timeline view groups photos by date. Select photos using the checkbox to perform bulk actions like Download or Email.

Select photos in the Timeline, then use Bulk actions → Email to send them directly from Procore.

5
Add a description and tag users
Click any photo to open the detail panel. Add a description with searchable keywords and tag users in the comments to highlight something specific.

Use the description field for searchable keywords. Tag users in comments to flag things directly to them.

6
Email photos directly from Procore
Use Bulk Actions → Email to send selected photos to any Procore user (internal or external) without leaving the platform.

Send to any Procore user - internally or externally - straight from the Photos tool. No Outlook required.

⚡Pro(core) Tips
🔑

Always upload as Private. Photos set to Private are still visible to all internal Tesla users, but won’t be shared externally unless you explicitly choose to share them.

🔍

Use descriptive keywords when adding photo descriptions - they make photos far easier to find when searching across hundreds of uploads.

📱

The Procore mobile app is available for both Apple and Android - great for taking and uploading site photos directly from the field.
