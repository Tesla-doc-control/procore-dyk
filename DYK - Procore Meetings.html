Procore’s Meetings Tool allows you to record, assign, and follow up on every agenda item discussed with internal teams, contractors, and clients - all within one space. Whether it’s weekly planning sessions, design workshops, commissioning meetings, or site coordination catchups, the Meetings tool helps ensure nothing is missed or left undocumented.

Why it matters
📋

Centralised meeting records

All agendas, notes, and attachments are kept inside the project - not scattered across inboxes or spreadsheets.

✅

Action items assigned during the meeting

Turn discussion points into real tasks. Assign owners, due dates, and track closure in real time.

↺

Automatic rollover of open items

Any unresolved items automatically continue to the next meeting - no manual copy/paste required.

📝

Versioned minutes with audit trail

Every revision of your minutes is recorded, ensuring full traceability and accountability across the project.

Key actions - straight from Procore

These are the actual Procore UI buttons you’ll use when working with Meetings - each plays a distinct role in keeping your records structured and complete.

1
Create a new meeting
Use this button to start a new meeting record. Set the date, attendees, and agenda items before the meeting begins.

Click + Create meeting from the Meetings tool to open a new meeting and begin building your agenda ahead of time.

2
Schedule a follow-up meeting
After your meeting, use this button to create the next occurrence - carrying forward all unresolved action items automatically.

The Follow-up meeting button creates the next meeting with open items pre-populated. Remember to update the date before you start!

How to use it effectively
1

Create your meeting agenda in advance

Set discussion topics, include documents or drawings, and add relevant attendees before the meeting starts.

2

Capture live notes during the meeting

Record key decisions and discussion outcomes in real time, straight into Procore - from any device.

3

Assign responsibilities on the spot

Add action item owners, due dates, and next steps directly during the meeting so nothing is forgotten.

4

Distribute minutes post-meeting

Share the agreed record automatically from Procore - ensuring every attendee has the same approved minutes.
