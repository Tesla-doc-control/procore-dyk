The Defect List tool in Procore (also known as Punchlisting) helps us quickly capture, track, and close out defects across our APAC projects. Defects can be raised directly in the field using a smartphone or tablet - complete with photos, location, and comments attached - ensuring accountability and transparency while giving leaders clear visibility of outstanding issues.

What the Defect List tool can do
📱

Raise defects from site

Create and assign defect items directly from site using your mobile device - no paperwork, no delays.

📎

Attach photos and documents

Add photos, drawings, or documents to each defect item to provide context and evidence for resolution.

⏱️

Track status and accountability

Set due dates, track status, and see average response times to keep teams on track and overdue items visible.

📊

Generate exportable reports

Export defect lists to share with stakeholders, provide closure evidence, and demonstrate project quality standards.

How to use it
1

Create a defect item in the field

Use the Procore mobile app to raise a defect on site. Add a description, location, and assign it to the responsible party.

2

Attach photos and drawings for context

Photograph the defect directly in the app, or attach relevant drawings and documents to give the assignee full context.

3

Set responsibility and due date

Assign the defect to the correct company or person and set a clear due date to ensure accountability and timely resolution.

4

Track status through to closure

Monitor progress via the dashboard. The assigned party responds in Procore and the item moves to Ready to Close for verification before final closure.

⚡Pro(core) Tips
📱

The Procore mobile app is the most powerful way to use the Defect List - raise items, attach photos, and update status directly from site without returning to a desk.

📎

Always attach a photo when raising a defect. It removes ambiguity, speeds up resolution, and provides irrefutable closure evidence when the item is marked complete.

📊

The exported reports from Procore make excellent client-facing quality documents - highlighting outstanding defects, providing closure evidence, and giving clear oversight across the portfolio. See the attached example reports from the Iberdrola Smithfield project.
