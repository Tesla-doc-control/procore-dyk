Within Procore Document Management, you can overlay two revisions of any drawing or document and see exactly what changed - highlighted automatically. No more manually comparing files side by side or hunting through revision clouds on a drawing.

Why it matters
⚡

Instant change detection

Spot design changes between revisions in seconds - no manual comparison needed.

🛡

Reduce construction risk

Verify you are working from the latest approved revision before work starts on site.

📋

Faster review cycles

Reviewers can focus on what actually changed - not re-read entire documents.

✅

Comment with confidence

Add markups directly on the comparison view and link them to your review comments.

See it in action - real project example

The screenshots below are taken directly from Procore on this project. Follow along to see exactly how the overlay comparison works from start to finish.

1
Open the document in Document Management
Navigate to Project → Document Management and click the document to compare. Here we open MUCHO&M-TESLA-ARC-DWG-0001 (Cover Sheet, Rev D).
2
Expand the revision history
Click the revision dropdown to see all versions. Version V2 Revision D is currently in review; Version V1 Revision C was Revise & Resubmit. The Compare button sits at the bottom.
3
Select the revision to compare against
A search panel opens showing the current document at the top. Select the older revision to overlay - here we choose Version 1 Revision C to compare against Version 2 Revision D.
4
Click Compare
Hit the orange Compare button to launch the overlay viewer. Procore processes both revisions and highlights every change automatically.
5
Instantly see what changed
The overlay view renders both drawings superimposed. All differences - added annotations, moved elements, revised notes - are highlighted in colour. The panel on the right identifies which revision is which.

Live overlay: MUCHO&M-TESLA-ARC-DWG-0001 - Revision C vs Revision D

💡 Pro Tips
🎯

Use during transmittal review: When a resubmission comes in (Status B / C response), run the overlay immediately to confirm only the agreed comments have been addressed - no surprise scope changes.

🔁

IFC check before construction: Compare the latest IFC revision against the previous approval-stage revision (e.g. Rev B vs Rev 0) to confirm all review comments were incorporated before issuing to site.

🖨

Export the comparison: You can save or print the overlay view as a PDF for inclusion in your review register or meeting minutes - great for audit trails.

📌

Works on all file types: PDFs, drawings (.dwg published to PDF), specifications, reports - any document uploaded to Document Management supports the overlay feature.
